Our Mission Statement

Roc Nation is a full-service management and entertainment company founded by JAY-Z to create the optimum environment for artists of all kinds so they can deliver their creative vision to fans worldwide.

The FC-Z programme is committed to empowering creative-fashion students through extensive education, and experience with our in-house brand Paper Planes. We hope to steer our interns onto the path of success for the long term and support them every step of the way.

In the year 2020, we witnessed many events that became a part of global history, these events inspired our design team and directors to be determined to see a change and send out a message of hope. Our programme seeks the opportunity to better the lives of hopeful individuals across the world. The aim is to help our students to be prepared for anything because they are the Fashion CreativeZ of the Future.
